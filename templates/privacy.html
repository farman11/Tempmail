Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority

At TempMail by Root Group Tech, we are committed to protecting your privacy. Our temporary email service is designed with privacy-first principles to ensure your personal information remains secure and anonymous.

Information We Collect

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or permanent email address.
  • Temporary Email Data: We temporarily store emails received by your disposable email address for up to 24 hours.
  • Technical Data: We may collect basic technical information such as IP addresses and browser information for security and service improvement purposes.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide temporary email services
  • Prevent spam and abuse
  • Improve our service quality
  • Ensure system security

Data Retention

All temporary emails and associated data are automatically deleted after 24 hours. We do not maintain long-term storage of any email content or user activities.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide our temporary email functionality. These services are bound by their own privacy policies and security standards.

Your Rights

  • Right to anonymity - no registration required
  • Right to automatic data deletion
  • Right to service without tracking

Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact Root Group Tech at our official website.
